Site Name

Rent & Build – This name represents our mission of helping people find affordable apartments worldwide while also exploring greener and smarter construction materials. It captures both the renting and building aspects of the project.

Optional domain: rentandbuild.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide affordable housing listings worldwide and help individuals explore sustainable construction materials with real market prices. It also serves as an educational hub for green building methods.

Scenarios

  • “Where can I find eco-friendly building materials with real-world prices?”
  • “What are the most affordable apartments available in my city right now?”
  • “How can I learn more about sustainable building methods before starting my project?”

Color Schema

The site will use two main colors:

  • Primary Color (#2E8B57): Used for headings, navigation highlights, and call-to-action buttons.
  • Secondary Color (#f4f4f4): Used for page background and content sections.
  • Accent Color 1 (#E6E49F): Used for subtle highlights and section dividers.
  • Accent Color 2 (#A4A13B): Used for emphasis in icons, buttons, or special sections.

Typography

Font Family: Montserrat (Google Fonts)

  • Headings: Montserrat, weight 600 (bold).
  • Body Text: Montserrat, weight 400 (regular).
  • Navigation/Menu: Montserrat, weight 600 for clarity and emphasis.
